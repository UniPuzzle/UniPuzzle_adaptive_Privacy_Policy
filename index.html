Privacy Policy

Information collection

Our site doesn’t collect any personally identifiable information from you.

Information you provide

We only collect information when you provide it. Currently it is only through the contacting form or sending us a direct email message.

Information on technology collected from you

We automatically collect some non-personally identifiable information about technology you use while browsing and interacting with our website. We do this to improve and adjust our site to meet your technological needs, and to make your user experience more convenient and human-friendly. This information includes but is not limited to: the domain from which you access the Internet; IP address; browser type; screen resolution; Internet connection speed; operating system; the date and time you access our site; and the Internet address of the website from which you linked to our website.

Usage of cookies

Presently we do not intentionally use cookies in any personally identifiable purpose. This can change in the future.

Usage of the obtained information

Presently we do not use the information which we obtain for you in any specific personally identifiable purpose. The information on technology you use is used for the analytical purposes to optimize your experience on our website. The contact information is only used for the communicating purposes directly with you.

Sharing of the information

We do not share the obtained information with third parties. There are two exceptions.
1. The information on technology you use is obtained via the third party module. This information is automatically available to that party.
2. In case legal requirements demand us to provide some information in compliance with the appropriate law, we will be forced to do that. At the same time we will do our best to provide as little information under such circumstances as the appropriate law will allows us to do so.

Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Links to third-party websites

Our website contains links to third-party websites. Their privacy policies may be different than ours. We do not have control over the information that is submitted to, or collected by these third parties. Please, consult the privacy polices of those websites to learn about their practices.
